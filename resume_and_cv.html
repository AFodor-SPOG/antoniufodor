Education

University of Toledo

Ph.D. in Physics | 2018 - 2025

I earned my PhD from the University of Toledo in August 2025. My dissertation was entitled "Major and Minor Pathways of Evolution: Suppressing Star Formation in Nearby Galaxies".

This work included:

  • Observational studies of post-starburst galaxies at low redshift using the DeVeny Spectrograph on the Lowell Discovery Telescope
  • Building a data reduction pipeline to clean the large collected sample of galaxies
  • Identifying outflows in PSB galaxies using long-slit optical spectroscopy

My PhD work has resulted in one peer-reviewed publication to date.
